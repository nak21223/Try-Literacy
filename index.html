Our Mission

TRY Literacy provides one-on-one remedial instruction in reading comprehension and basic math for students in Grades 3–8. We help learners rebuild foundational skills, confidence, and intrinsic motivation.

Explore Our Services

Our Core Instruction Areas

Reading Comprehension

  • Identifying the Main Idea and Supporting Details
  • Making Inferences and Predictions
  • Understanding Vocabulary in Context

Basic Math Skills

  • Mastery of Operations (Addition through Division)
  • Fractions, Decimals, and Percent Concepts
  • Problem-Solving and Pre-Algebraic Thinking

Who We Serve

Grades 3–5

Developing strong reading fluency and math foundations to prepare for upper elementary learning success.

Grades 6–8

Filling gaps in comprehension and math reasoning to ensure readiness for high school-level courses.

Common Questions

What grades do you tutor?

TRY Literacy focuses on students in Grades 3–8 who need remedial support in reading and math.

Are sessions one-on-one?

Yes! Every tutoring session is individualized and customized for your child’s specific learning needs.

How long are sessions?

Standard sessions are 45 minutes—perfect for maintaining focus and engagement online.
